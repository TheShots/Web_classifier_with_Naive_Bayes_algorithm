    Welcome to the text document classifier!

    This application lets you classify documents according to categories, specified by you. Categorization is accomplished via an adaptation of the Naive Bayes algorithm which is based on applying Bayes' theorem with strong (naive) independence assumptions between the features, occuring in the provided documents.

    The documents to be classified are currently restricted to PDF format. To upload documents for categorization, press the "Add Documents" and select the desired ones from your machine. Multiple categories can be supplied simultaneously. To remove an already uploaded document, select it from the list and press "Remove Documents".

    When you have supplied all documents, press the "Classify!" button. After a short processing phase you'll be presented with a download link. Click on the link to download your categorization results.
